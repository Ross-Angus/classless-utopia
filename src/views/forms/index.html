Forms

Vertical layouts of form fields in a single column can be achieved with the following markup:

<form method="get" action="#">
  <span>
    <p>
      <label for="name">What is your first name?</label>
      <input name="first-name" id="name">
    </p>
    <p>
      <button>Submit</button>
    </p>
  </span>
</form>

Because a horizontal layout automatically occurs when a paragraph is the immediate child of either a form or a fieldset element, a span tag is used as a buffer to break this behaviour.

Avoid using a div tag instead as this will trigger a multi-column layout.
